**Major Phase:** Outbreak and Escalation

The fire broke out in the afternoon. Fueled by strong winds and flammable scaffolding, it spread rapidly to the top floor. The Fire Services Department upgraded the fire to Level 5, mobilizing resources across Hong Kong for rescue. One senior firefighter was confirmed dead on the first day.
